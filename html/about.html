Sam Zhang is currently a software engineer at Pivotal Labs, where he helps companies build software engineering teams.

Formerly, he worked on rapid prototyping data science tools for Memex, a DARPA project for finding human trafficking on the Internet.

He is an Eric & Wendy Schmidt Data Science for Social Good Fellow. He talked about it with "fellow fellow" Peter Landwehr on WBEZ TechShift. He also shared air-time on NPR Marketplace with Matt Conway's pie.

Some social media links are my Github, Twitter, and last.fm.
