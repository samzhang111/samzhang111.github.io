Sam Zhang

I am a PhD student in the Department of Applied Mathematics at the University of Colorado at Boulder, and a NSF Graduate Research Fellow. My advisor is Aaron Clauset.

Prior to graduate school, I worked for four years as a software engineer at companies such as OpenCounter and Pivotal Labs.

I received my undergraduate degree in Computer Science and Psychology from Swarthmore College in 2014. I was a 2014 University of Chicago Data Science for Social Good Fellow.

My hobbies include making interactive mathematical toys and taking my long dog on adventures, and as of late, biking.

Pronouns: he/him.
