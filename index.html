Sam Zhang

I will be starting a PhD in the Department of Applied Mathematics at the University of Colorado, Boulder in Fall 2019.

My interest is at the intersection of data science with networks, graphs, dynamics, and topology. Some themes for me are:

  • 1) expanding the mathematical tools used in data science, and
  • 2) applying data science toward socially impactful problems.

I received B.A.'s in Computer Science and Psychology from Swarthmore College in 2014. I was also a 2014 University of Chicago Data Science for Social Good Fellow.

Prior to becoming a math person, I worked at OpenCounter and Pivotal Labs.

My hobbies include scraping unusual datasets and making mathematical visualizations. Also, I enjoy taking my long dog on adventures.
