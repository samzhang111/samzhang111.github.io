Sam Zhang's Homepage

I am a PhD student and NSF Graduate Research Fellow in the Department of Applied Mathematics at the University of Colorado at Boulder. My advisor is Aaron Clauset.

I received my undergraduate degree in Computer Science and Psychology from Swarthmore College in 2014. I was a 2014 University of Chicago Data Science for Social Good Fellow. Then I worked as a software engineer at companies such as OpenCounter and Pivotal Labs.

My hobbies include making interactive mathematical toys (see thumbnails or blog posts below) and taking my long dog on adventures, and as of late, biking.

Pronouns: he/him.
