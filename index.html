Sam Zhang's Homepage

I am a PhD student and NSF Graduate Research Fellow in the Department of Applied Mathematics at the University of Colorado at Boulder, and currently a research intern with the Computational Social Science group at Microsoft Research NYC. My advisor is Aaron Clauset.

My research is highly interdisciplinary, where I combine mathematics with my experience as a software engineer to study important issues facing society. I also love making mathematical visualizations, many of which are on display below.
