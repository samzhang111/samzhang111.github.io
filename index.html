Sam Zhang

I am a PhD student in the Department of Applied Mathematics at the University of Colorado, Boulder. My advisor is Aaron Clauset.

Prior to graduate school, I worked for four years as a software engineer at companies such as OpenCounter and Pivotal Labs.

I received B.A.'s in Computer Science and Psychology from Swarthmore College in 2014. I was also a 2014 University of Chicago Data Science for Social Good Fellow.

My hobbies include making mathematical visualizations and taking my long dog on adventures.

Pronouns: he/him.
