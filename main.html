Sam Zhang's Homepage

I am an Applied Complexity postdoc at the Santa Fe Institute, and starting in Fall 2025, an Assistant Professor of Statistics affiliated with the Vermont Complex Systems Institute at the University of Vermont.

I received my PhD in Applied Mathematics from the University of Colorado Boulder, advised by Aaron Clauset and Dan Larremore, where I was supported by an NSF Graduate Research Fellowship. During my PhD, I worked as a PhD research intern with the MSR NYC Computational Social Science group and the Human Rights Data Analysis Group.

More about me here.
